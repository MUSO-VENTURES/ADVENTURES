Quick questions

Do coins expire?
No — once you have coins, they're yours until you spend them.
Do unlocks expire?
One-time unlocks (search range, extra stops, real venue search, themes) are permanent once purchased. MUSO Pass perks last as long as your subscription is active.
What's the difference between buying coins and subscribing?
Buying a pack is a one-time top-up you can use however you like. MUSO Pass is a monthly plan that hands you a fresh batch of coins automatically and keeps certain unlocks active the whole time you're subscribed — better value if you play often.
Can I just play for free?
Yes. Every player starts with a free 30-mile range, 3 free stops per adventure, and can reach real venue search for free at Level 5 just by playing. Coins are there if you want to go further, faster.

Prices shown in USD. Adventure Coins and MUSO Pass are used only within MUSO Adventures and have no cash value outside the app.
